About Campus Live Dashboard

What is Campus Live Dashboard?

Campus Live Dashboard is a real-time platform that allows students to track the availability of shared resources across the college campus. Our goal is to help students save time and make informed decisions about where to go on campus.

Features

  • Real-time updates on library occupancy
  • Canteen queue length monitoring
  • Washing machine availability in hostels
  • Sports facility status
  • User contributions and voting system

How It Works

Students can view the current status of various campus resources and contribute by updating information based on their observations. The platform uses a voting system to ensure accuracy and reliability of the information.

Our Team

This platform was developed by students for students. We're constantly working to improve the experience and welcome feedback and contributions from the campus community.
